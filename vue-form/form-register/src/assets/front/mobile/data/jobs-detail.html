Customer Care Officer

Job Information

Remuneration structure:

  • Develop and periodically review remuneration structure
  • Periodically review job specification, conduct job matching and salary survey
  • Update salary scale
  • Assist CBM to develop human resources budget and periodically review the HR budget
  • Report on monthly employee cost

Cash compensation and Non-cash benefits:

  • Prepare annual salary review
  • Proceed monthly payroll calculation and relevant cash compensations to Vietnamese, expatriate staff, teachers and teaching assistants
  • Ensure all compensations to comply with government and ILA’s regulations.
  • Claim and pay allowances from Social Insurance Department, Insurance Company
  • Update all staff about one’s eligible non-cash benefits
  • Update all staff about one’s eligible non-cash benefits

Leave management:

  • Monitor all types of leave in leave management system for Vietnamese & expatriate staff

Others:

  • Complete all reports for external authorities such as Social Insurance Dept, Police Dept, Dolisa, Tax Dept and internal report system [ie cost monitoring report, employee statistic report, headcount report]
  • Provide remuneration strategic inputs
  • Execute and monitor HR budget
  • Effectively update to staff internal regulations, employee handbook, new governmental regulations

Job Requirement
  • Bachelors Degree from an accredited University
  • Already at the same position with at least 2 years experience in compensation and benefits management
  • Multinational organization experience preferred (in the education, consumer or financial industry is a plus)
  • Proficient in remuneration best practice, Vietnamese labour code, governmental regulations relating to human resources
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to liaise and negotiate at executive level and above in a professional manner
  • Strong leadership skills and cross cultural understanding
